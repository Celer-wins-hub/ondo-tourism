Welcome to Ondo State Tourism

Where Nature Meets Culture in Perfect Harmony

The Ondo State Ministry of Tourism is dedicated to showcasing the natural wonders, cultural treasures, and warm hospitality that make our state a premier destination in Nigeria. From the ancient Idanre Hills to the historic Owo Museum, our state offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

Known as the "Sunshine State," Ondo boasts lush forests, stunning waterfalls, pristine beaches along the Atlantic coast, and vibrant festivals that celebrate our rich Yoruba heritage.

Top Destinations

Explore some of the most breathtaking locations Ondo State has to offer

Idanre Hills

A UNESCO World Heritage tentative site featuring 680 steps, ancient dwellings, and panoramic views of the surrounding valleys.

📍 Idanre LGA  |  ⭐ Top Rated

Owo Museum of Antiquities

Home to priceless 15th-century bronze sculptures, terracotta works, and artifacts from the ancient Owo Kingdom.

📍 Owo LGA  |  🏛️ Cultural Site

Igbo Olodumare

A mystical forest grove surrounded by lush vegetation, believed to be a sacred spiritual site rich in folklore.

📍 Oke-Igbo  |  🌿 Nature Reserve

Rich Cultural Heritage

Experience the vibrant traditions that make Ondo State unique

🎭

Ogun Festival

Annual celebration honoring the god of iron with colorful masquerade displays and traditional rites.

👑

Olowo's Palace

The historic palace of the Olowo of Owo, featuring exquisite courtyards and royal artifacts spanning centuries.

🥁

Traditional Music & Dance

Experience the rhythmic beats of talking drums, bata, and the graceful ekun iyawo bridal dance.

🎨

Aso Oke Weaving

Hand-woven textiles crafted by skilled artisans, representing the pinnacle of Yoruba fashion artistry.
